Logo

These logo designs were made for my photography site. My style is inspired by landscapes and human connection. The tree symbolizes the outdoors while the circle with my business name represents connection and loyalty. I wanted the logo to have a free and flowing feel to it which is why I decided on a more whimsical tree rather than a geometric one.

Main Color Logo

This is the primary logo to be used on all media of varying backgrounds.

  • Background Color: #cbc1b4;
  • Lettering and Shape Color: #ffffff;
  • Opacity: 100%;
  • Font Family: Cabin, humanist sans-serif

Black & White

This variation of the logo is best used for white or light backgrounds.

  • Background Color: #000000;
  • Lettering and Shape Color: #ffffff;
  • Opacity: 100%;
  • Font Family: Cabin, humanist sans-serif

Black & White Reverse

This variation of the logo is best used on dark backgrounds.
